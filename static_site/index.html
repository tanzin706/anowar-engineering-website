About Us

AKIJ Light Engineering Limited is a forward-thinking and innovative company, established in 2024, with a vision to redefine quality and reliability in its industry. The company is gearing up to make its mark by offering a diverse range of high-quality products, including electrical lighting & accessories, home appliances, building materials, hardware tools etc.

With a focus on sustainability and excellence, we’re committed to providing solutions that blend seamlessly into everyday life, making it smarter, easier, and more efficient. We aim to create products that not only meet but exceed expectations, ensuring a positive impact on the lives of our customers and the world around us.

What truly sets us apart is our culture, built on a foundation of core values: Positivity, Teamwork, Ownership, Customer Centricity, and Service Orientation. These principles guide every decision we make and every action we take. Our goal is simple yet ambitious—to become the industry’s gold standard, the go-to choice for customers who value quality, innovation, and trust.

Behind our success there is a passionate and driven leadership team, committed to pushing boundaries and delivering exceptional value. We believe in putting our customers first, embracing innovation and maintaining unwavering standards of quality. Whether you are a homeowner, a builder or a business professional, we are here to provide solutions that work for you.

Our Achievements

Diverse Product Range: Offers a wide array of products, including lighting solutions, switch accessories, home appliances, cables, and hardware tools, catering to both residential and commercial needs.

Customer-Centric Support: Provides excellent after-sales service and technical support for all product categories, ensuring customer satisfaction.
